Detailed Description

template<typename ptr_type>
class adiar::internal::__uid< ptr_type >

A unique identifier a decision diagram node.

This essentially is a ptr guaranteed to point to a node, i.e. it is never NIL, and without any associated information, e.g. without a flag.
